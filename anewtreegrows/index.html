A NEW TREE GROWS

    A New Tree Grows is a work in progress, mixing gameplay with audiovisual storytelling.
    It is centred around a fantastical story of a future world in which architects and smart intelligent robots work together to create sustainable cities.
    It is designed to be seen on a desktop computer and experiencing a 3D-soundscape with plugged in headphones.
    It will later be shown at international festivals for digital art as well as distributed online.

    Technical prototype

    The current prototype describes the visual flow through the story and the game, narrated with a voiceover
    Later developments involve:

    Music and soundeffects
    A more advanced animation scene
    Modeling, Texturing and Lightdesign
    UI development
    Deployment with a customized domain
