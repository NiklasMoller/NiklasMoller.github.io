A New Tree Grows was developed duing 2020-2021 as a non-profit production. It was inspired by how new technology makes and unmakes new forms of expressions, with emphasis on the research carried out in modern architecture on sustainability and 3D-printing technologies.
Directed by: Niklas Möller
Written by: Carolina Nilsson
Music by: August Norborg
Texturing and Lighs by: Emal Ghouse
Voice by: Robert Bolin
Game development by: Niklas Möller
The work has been designed for festivals and exhibitions.
